Services that we offer under "Labour Law"

A Factory License, governed by the Factories Act, 1948, is mandatory for premises engaging a specified number of workers. It ensures the health, safety, and welfare of workers, regulating various aspects of factory operations, including working conditions, safety measures, and facilities.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) streamlines the Factory License process, offering precise application support, expert compliance guidance, seamless renewal assistance, audit preparation, and dedicated legal representation. By choosing GCA, clients secure a reliable partner for a compliant and thriving industrial setup.

Creating a factory plan in accordance with rules involves meticulous consideration of legal and regulatory requirements, covering aspects such as site selection, structural design, environmental compliance, workforce management, and safety measures. This ensures that the factory operates within the framework of laws governing industrial establishments.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) streamlines this process by providing regulatory expertise, assisting in documentation, ensuring comprehensive compliance, and offering strategies for risk mitigation. With GCA's guidance, clients can establish a legally compliant factory plan, focusing on operational success and growth.

A "Stability Certificate" typically refers to a document issued by a qualified structural engineer or authority certifying the stability and safety of a structure, such as a building or bridge. This certificate is often required to ensure that the structure meets prescribed safety standards and can withstand the anticipated loads and environmental conditions.GCA provides expertise in obtaining Stability Certificates, facilitating structural assessments, coordinating with qualified engineers, offering documentation support, and ensuring regulatory compliance for the safety and stability of structures.

A "Leave Policy" outlines an organization's guidelines regarding employee leave entitlements, procedures for requesting and approving leave, and the terms and conditions associated with different types of leaves.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) assists organizations in crafting Leave Policies that align with legal standards, ensuring fairness and compliance. Our expertise lies in tailoring policies to specific needs, communicating them effectively to employees, and providing guidance on dispute resolution, contributing to a positive and legally sound work environment

"Safety and Health" in the workplace refer to measures and practices aimed at ensuring the well-being of employees by creating a secure and healthy work environment. This encompasses protocols for preventing accidents, providing emergency response procedures, maintaining occupational health standards, and promoting overall employee wellness.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) specializes in ensuring workplace safety and health compliance. From safety regulations to emergency response planning and employee wellness programs, GCA facilitates a secure and healthy work environment.

The term "Child Labour Act" typically refers to legislation aimed at preventing and regulating the employment of children. In the context of India, the Child Labour (Prohibition and Regulation) Act, 1986, is a crucial piece of legislation. This act prohibits the engagement of children in certain occupations and regulates the conditions of work for children in permissible occupations.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) specializes in ensuring organizations adhere to the Child Labour Act. We provide guidance on legal compliance, assist in policy development, offer documentation support, and promote awareness programs to prevent child labour and maintain regulatory compliance.

Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) specializes in ensuring regulatory compliance with acts such as the National Building Code and the Building and Other Construction Workers Act. We offer documentation support, contractual guidance, and assistance in implementing safety measures to secure legal adherence in building and construction projects.
