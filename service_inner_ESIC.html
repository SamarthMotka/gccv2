Services that we offer under "ESIC"

ESIC Registration is a crucial step for employers to provide social security benefits to their employees. The Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) manages this program, offering medical and cash benefits to employees and their dependents in case of sickness, maternity, disablement, or death due to employment-related injuries.Choosing Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) for ESIC Registration brings expertise to your fingertips. Our seasoned team simplifies the registration process, ensuring compliance with ESIC regulations. We handle the documentation, liaise with ESIC authorities, and streamline the entire registration journey.

Employee Insurance Number (EIN) is a unique identification assigned to an individual for their insurance and benefits coverage. It plays a crucial role in managing and tracking an employee's insurance-related details, ensuring they receive the entitled benefits. The process involves obtaining and assigning a distinct EIN to each employee, a vital aspect of workforce management.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) simplifies the Employee Insurance Number generation process. Our expert team ensures a seamless and efficient approach to assigning unique EINs to employees.

Monthly ESIC Challan Preparation is a vital aspect of ensuring compliance with the Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) regulations. This process involves calculating and preparing the monthly contribution challan, which includes employer and employee contributions towards the ESIC scheme. Accurate preparation of these challans is essential for providing social security benefits to employees.Our experienced team manages the intricate details, calculates contributions accurately, and ensures timely submission.

Monthly ESIC Return Filing is a crucial aspect of compliance with the Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) regulations. This process involves submitting detailed reports on employee contributions, ensuring accurate documentation of their social security benefits. Timely and precise filing is essential for maintaining ESIC compliance. Our expert team manages the process efficiently, handling the necessary documentation and ensuring accurate submission of reports.

Monthly TIC (Temporary Identification Certificate) Generation is a crucial step in managing employee records and compliance. This process involves creating temporary identification certificates for employees, providing a unique identifier for their contributions and benefits. Accurate TIC generation ensures that employees receive the entitlements they deserve.Let GCA handle the TIC generation, allowing you to focus on your business's core operations with confidence.

The Employee Insurance Card, commonly known as the Pehchaan Card, is a crucial identification document for employees covered under insurance schemes. This card serves as proof of enrollment in various employee benefit programs, including health and life insurance. It typically contains essential details such as the employee's name, photograph, insurance policy details, and other relevant information. Our experienced team ensures accurate documentation, quick processing, and the efficient creation of Pehchaan Cards for employees. By choosing GCA, you not only guarantee compliance with insurance regulations but also provide your workforce with tangible proof of their insurance coverage.

The Employee Accidental Death Claim process is designed to provide financial assistance to the dependents or beneficiaries of an employee in the unfortunate event of accidental death during employment. This service involves thorough documentation, submission of necessary forms, and liaising with relevant authorities to ensure a prompt and compassionate settlement of the claim. Our dedicated team takes charge of the intricate details, facilitating a smooth and empathetic claims process. By choosing GCA, you not only ensure timely and accurate claim submissions but also provide support to the affected families during a challenging time.
