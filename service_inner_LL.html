In the Indian context, the term "Construction Act" is not a specific standalone legislation. Instead, the construction industry is governed by a combination of laws and regulations at both the central and state levels. These regulations cover various aspects such as labor, safety, contractual obligations, and environmental considerations.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) offers comprehensive services tailored to the diverse needs of the construction industry in India. Our expertise extends across relevant legal and regulatory frameworks, ensuring clients receive adept guidance and support in navigating the complexities of the construction sector.

The "Maternity Act" typically refers to the Maternity Benefit Act, 1961, in the context of Indian labor laws. The Maternity Benefit Act is designed to regulate the employment of women in certain establishments for a certain period before and after childbirth and to provide them with maternity benefits. Benifits like Maternity Leave, Medical Bonus, Prohibition of Dismissal etc. are covered under this act.Girish Chaudhary Associates (GCA) offers specialized services to help clients navigate the Maternity Benefit Act and ensure compliance with its provisions.
