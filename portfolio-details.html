Provident Fund

Services that we offer under the domain of Provident Fund

  • PF Registration:PF registration offers a robust mechanism for employees to build a financial cushion for their future. By contributing a portion of their salary to the Provident Fund, employees create a savings pool that serves as a reliable resource during retirement or unforeseen circumstances.
  • Employee UAN Generation:The Universal Account Number (UAN) is a unique identifier assigned to each employee contributing to the Employee Provident Fund (EPF) in India. UAN simplifies and centralizes PF-related activities for employees.
  • Monthly PF Challan Preparation:Preparing monthly Provident Fund (PF) challans is a vital aspect of statutory compliance for businesses in India. It involves the calculation and submission of PF contributions for both employers and employees.
  • Monthly PF Return Filling:Monthly PF return filling is a vital step for business compliance as it involves crucial tasks like PF ECR preparation, bifucation of basic salary, deaness alloowance and PF calculations, submission of ECR on EPFO portal and recordkeeping.
  • Employee Death Claim:In the unfortunate event of an employee's demise, dealing with the ensuing formalities, especially regarding the Provident Fund (PF), requires a compassionate and informed approach. We thoroughly understand the sentiment of our clients in such kind of unfortunate events and try to do our best to keep their needs above ours.
  • PF Department Liasoning:GCC acts as a communication bridge between your organization and the PF department, facilitating clear and efficient exchange of information. At GCC, our dedicated team of experts provides PF department liaisoning services tailored to your organization's needs.
  • PF Department Audit Conduct:GCC conducts a comprehensive review of your organization's PF records and documentation to identify and rectify potential compliance gaps before the audit.
